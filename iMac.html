The iMac
A series of the personal computer Macintosh, the iMac became Apple's fastest selling computer when relseased. The iMac is said to have saved the financial trubles that Apple was in. It was an inexpensive design that would easily allow customers to get connected to the internet. It costed $1,299 in 1998 and would now be worth $2,500 today.
